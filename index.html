About Jess

I'm Jess. Mom of four (including twins), Certified Parenting Educator, and the mom who watched her daughter start to flinch when I walked into a room.

I was pregnant with my twins when I hit rock bottom. My body was exhausted. My hormones were a wildfire. My four-year-old, this once brave, sparkly, confident little girl, had gone quiet and closed. My one-year-old son wasn't even getting a real parent. Just a shell of one. I remember lying in bed one night, already dreading the next day, feeling like my kids needed to be saved from me.

And then something shifted. No one is coming to raise my kids. I have to become what they need.

That moment sent me down a path I'll never stop walking. I studied everything. I spent years teaching in schools across different cultures, including Thailand, where I first witnessed what truly connected families looked like. I trained formally as a Parenting Educator. I developed a critical eye for what mainstream Western parenting gets profoundly wrong. And I found ancient wisdom from cultures around the world that confirmed what I was learning: the natural way is so much easier. We just have to unlearn almost everything we've been taught.

Our home transformed. I stopped surviving and started genuinely loving my kids again. Now I teach other mamas the same shift. Not more tips to pile on. A completely different framework. One that actually works. 🧡
